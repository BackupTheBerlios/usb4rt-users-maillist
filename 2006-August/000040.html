FYI:

On  my Dell laptop inspiron 600m, the video driver labeled radeon at pci 
(from cat /proc/interrupts) was interacting with the module 
rt_uhci_hcd.ko and my system was freezing
Unfortunately, the module for the radeon could not be unloaded because 
it was in use.

To fix the IRQ conflict, one needs to recompile the kernel, making sure 
that any reference to the radeon in the .config file is commented out.

I am not sure of what radeon at pci does exactly because my laptop's video 
card seems to be working just fine without it...

On a desktop, this type of IRQ conflict with a video card can sometimes 
be fixed by simply switching the video card to another PCI slot (I tried 
on a desktop computer and it worked).

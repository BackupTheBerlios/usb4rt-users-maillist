After putting on the side for a few weeks my project that makes use of
usb4rt,  I am now coming back to it. Right before putting everything on the

under bulk transfer. One had to do with a weird restriction on the URB size
and the other one with a faulty read operation. These problems were somehow
- i.e. to the best of my capabilities - reported on this mailing list in
September 2006.  Unfortunately, I never succeeded in finding what was wrong,
